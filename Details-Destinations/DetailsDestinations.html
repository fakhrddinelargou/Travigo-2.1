Top Places to Visit in Casablanca

Hassan II Mosque

The Hassan II Mosque is one of the largest mosques in Africa and a symbol of Casablanca. It is partly built over the Atlantic Ocean, offering stunning ocean views. Its minaret rises 210 meters, the tallest in the world. The mosque features intricate mosaics, hand-carved marble, and a retractable roof. It can host up to 100,000 worshippers and showcases Moroccan craftsmanship.A must-visit landmark for architecture, culture, and photography lovers.
