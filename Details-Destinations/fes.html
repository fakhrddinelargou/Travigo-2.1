Top Places to Visit in Fes

Fes Medina

The Fes Medina is one of the largest and oldest medinas in Morocco. Its narrow alleys and bustling souks are filled with traditional crafts, spices, and textiles. The medina is a UNESCO World Heritage site, preserving centuries of Moroccan culture. Visitors can explore historic monuments, mosques, and artisan workshops along the winding streets. It offers a vibrant atmosphere full of colors, smells, and sounds unique to Morocco. A must-visit for those seeking authentic culture, shopping, and photography opportunities.
